Sophisticated Estate Planning for High Net Worth Individuals

Significant wealth may present unique difficulties in the realm of estate planning. A high net worth individual requires a sophisticated plan that avoids probate and addresses asset preservation, property distribution, estate tax, retirement funding and charitable planning. At Melnik Law Group, PLLC, we have the capability to address the many issues that may arise in these matters. Our team of professionals and support staff are here to assist you in clarifying your immediate and long-term goals and making these a reality through strategic estate planning. We represent clients in Manhattan, Brooklyn and throughout New York City and offer a confidential consultation to discuss your concerns and how we can assist you.

High net worth estate planning requires a multi-level approach that your attorney and the other professionals at our law firm can provide. We will work to draft a will that conforms to your particular wishes in regard to your estate. We will help you create trusts that protect your wealth through careful tax planning, spendthrift protection and a plan for long term care. We will address issues related to mental capacity and legal competence to ensure that your wishes are carried out and assets safeguarded should you be unable to make these decisions yourself. Your personal goals and peace of mind are at the forefront of our concerns.
