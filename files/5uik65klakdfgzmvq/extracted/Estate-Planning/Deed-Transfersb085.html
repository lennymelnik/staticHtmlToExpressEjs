New York City Deed Transfer Attorney

Deed Transfers

For many people, their home is their largest asset. It is therefore an extremely important issue to address in creating a workable estate plan. A New York City estate planning attorney at Melnik Law Group, PLLC can talk to you about completing a deed transfer as part of your estate plan. This can help you for various reasons: to avoid taxation, ensure your property is transferred to a loved one, or help ensure that this property is not affected by creditor claims or Medicaid asset recovery. With attorneys, CPAs and skilled support staff to develop a multi-faceted approach to your estate plan and addressing your real property, our firm can help you make the right choices about a deed transfer.

Our law firm helps clients of all ages and financial situations in Brooklyn, Manhattan and throughout New York City. We welcome you to contact our offices by phone or online to undergo a confidential consultation regarding your particular case and concerns.

Transferring Your Manhattan or Brooklyn Home

There are various financial, legal and tax-related issues that may arise in regard to a deed transfer of any New York City area home. These issues must be taken into account in order to avoid negatively impacting Medicaid eligibility or even the possibility of losing the property to taxes. You most likely want your home to pass on to your children or other loved ones rather than lose it to the government. Careful planning is required to ensure that a deed transfer is completed properly, whether this is into a trust or is accomplished by transferring the property outright.

Contact a New York City deed transfer lawyer at our law offices to talk about how your home or other real estate may be affected by your estate plan. We are here to help and support you.
