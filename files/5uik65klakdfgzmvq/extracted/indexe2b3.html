Our law firm provides highly experienced legal counsel in all areas of estate and probate law. Our primary areas of practice in this regard include:

Estate Planning - Regardless of your age, planning for the future is an important step to take. This applies whether you need to draft a will that will simply divide few assets or if you are a high net worth individual who needs a sophisticated plan involving irrevocable trusts, life care planning, charitable planning and more. An attorney at our New York City law offices can provide you with the guidance and legal counsel you need.

Estate Administration and Probate - Upon the passing of a loved one, estate administration and probate matters may be a complex and emotionally involved situation to deal with. Our firm can help you close out your administration or probate matter as swiftly and smoothly as possible, avoiding unnecessary time and expense. We will work with you to resolve this matter and protect the estate, as needed, from creditor claims and discontented beneficiaries.

Estate Litigation/Probate Litigation - In some cases, litigation will be the only option in a matter involving a loved one's estate. A New York City estate lawyer at our firm will fight for your rights in these situations, working tirelessly to protect your share of the estate.
